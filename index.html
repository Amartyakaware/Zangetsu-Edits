Intro

Professional Video Editor specializing in high-retention short-form content. I create cinematic edits for football, anime, movies, gaming, and creators, combining smooth transitions, dynamic pacing, and impactful sound design to keep audiences engaged from the first second to the last.

Work

I create professional video edits designed to capture attention and leave a lasting impact. Specializing in football, anime, movies, series, gaming, and cinematic content, I transform raw footage into visually compelling stories through smooth transitions, dynamic pacing, creative effects, color grading, and immersive sound design. Whether it's a 30-second reel or a full-length video, every project is crafted with precision, creativity, and attention to detail to ensure the highest quality.

About

Hi, I'm the editor behind Zangetsu Edits. I specialize in creating high-quality short-form and long-form videos that are designed to capture attention and tell compelling stories. From football highlights and anime edits to movies, gaming, and cinematic content, I focus on smooth transitions, creative storytelling, precise pacing, and immersive sound design. Every project is approached with attention to detail and a commitment to delivering content that exceeds expectations.
